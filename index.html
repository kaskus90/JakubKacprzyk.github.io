Who I Am

I'm an aspiring Automation & Robotics engineer with a deep interest in artificial intelligence and the future of intelligent machines. My goal is to design systems where software intelligence meets physical hardware — from robotic arms on factory floors to autonomous vehicles navigating real-world environments.

I'm currently building my foundation in Python, ROS (Robot Operating System), computer vision, and machine learning. I believe the engineers who will shape the next decade are those who can bridge the gap between AI research and real physical systems.

Outside engineering I'm interested in simulation environments — including game engines — as platforms for training autonomous agents and testing automated systems at scale before deploying to real hardware.
